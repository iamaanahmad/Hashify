# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful**: Use inclusive language and listen to different viewpoints
- **Being supportive**: Help other community members and be patient with learning
- **Being collaborative**: Work together constructively toward common goals
- **Being constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Being honest**: Communicate openly and transparently
- **Giving credit**: Acknowledge the contributions of others

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- **Harassment**: Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated
- **Discrimination**: Discrimination based on any characteristic will not be tolerated
- **Trolling**: Intentional provocation or disruptive comments
- **Spam**: Repetitive or promotional messages
- **Abuse**: Any form of personal attacks or abuse
- **Sharing private information**: Doxxing or sharing others' private information without consent
- **Sexual harassment**: Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- **Violence**: Threats of violence or violent language

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Direct Contact**: Email [conduct@cit.org.in](mailto:conduct@cit.org.in)
2. **GitHub**: Report via [GitHub Issues](../../issues) with details
3. **Anonymous**: Use an anonymous reporting form if available

### Investigation Process

1. We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours
2. An investigation will be conducted promptly
3. We will maintain confidentiality as much as possible
4. We will keep you updated on the outcome

### Consequences

Community members who do not follow the Code of Conduct may face:

- Warning
- Temporary ban from the community
- Permanent ban from the community
- Legal action if necessary

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior
- Removing, editing, or rejecting comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct
- Banning temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub, issues, discussions, pull requests, etc.)
- Public spaces when representing the project
- Events organized by the project
- Any interaction related to the project

---

## Diversity and Inclusion

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We actively encourage:

- Participation from people of all backgrounds
- Diverse perspectives and experiences
- Equal opportunities for all contributors
- Accessibility and inclusivity in all communications
